Work

Here I share some of my data projects.

Amazon Fine Food Reviews Sentiment Analysis
The purpose of the project is to do basic Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and make prediction based on reviews as whether the review is Positive or Negative. Sentiment analysis of reviews/feedbacks helps the companies to improve Business Intelligence and Compatative Advantage. Also knowing their customers feelings about the product or services, companies can enhance customer experience and develop more appealing marketing strategies.

News Headline Classifier
In this project I attempted to build multilabel classification models to identify the category of a given news title. The datasetretrieved from Kaggleand it contains 422,937 news stories collected by a web aggregator between March 10th, 2014 and August 10th, 2014.

Credit Card Fraud Transaction Detector
In this project I created several classification models and tested how precise they identify a credit card fraud. For this purpose I used the dataset presenting two days credit card transactions by European cardholders made in September 2013.

Time Series: Statistical Modeling to Predict Google Stock Prices
Using daily stock closing prices for 12 years, I performed statistical modeling and applied machine learning to predict Google stock prices for the next 5 years. As an ML tool I used Facebook Prophet package. The next step in this project is to apply LSTM and compare the results.

Statistical Inference on Darwin's Finches
This project is about the search for statistical prove that the beak depth of the finch species called Scandens has changed over 40 years and the evolution of these birds in the tiny isolated island really happened.

European Social Survey Sample Testing
The European Social Survey (ESS) is an academically-driven multi-country survey, which has been administered in over 30 countries to date. The goal is to compare these samples to answer several questions such as "Did people become happier from 2012 to 2014" or "How often people in different countries met socially in 2014. Are there differences, and if so, which countries stand out?".

About

As a human being, I love to apply thought models to analyze different variables (let say pieces of information) collected by our brain (visually or as an auido) and achieve various concluions. With the background in Economics and professional experience in Digital Marketing, data was unattachable part of my life. After moving to the US, I was able to realize one of my wishes - to be able to write a solid code in one of programming languages. Python was the choice and I'm thankful to Python community for the oppotunities they have created which changes lifes. My deep interest in python took me to the point where I explored the world of Data Science. I have become extremely interested in advanced analytics, statistics, and machine learning. I started my self-learning journey to understand how can I solve real-world problems using data. Later I enrolled in Thinkful, an online bootcamp offering an intensive and comprehensive data science program to build off of my professional data analytics experience.
And here I am - exploring various datasets, learning ways and methods which can be applied to data to get desirable results. My interest and experience include Natural Language Processing, Time Series, Statistical Modeling and Image Classification. Working with data is a kind of art, a way of discovering hidden gems to see the big picture and understand the whole story that data wants to tell us. I can use data to make my own "story of the future" or "..past". I feel very excited about being a part of the Data Science community and see how AI applications change our world.
